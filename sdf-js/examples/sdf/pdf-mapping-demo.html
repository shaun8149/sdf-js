pdf → 3d.

Atlas M0.3 PDF parser + M1.5 slide-to-scene mapper, end-to-end. We pre-parsed a real 20-slide PowerPoint template into SlideData[], then the browser runs the SlideData → SceneData mapper for each slide and raymarches the result. Slide 18 has 10 percentage labels in a 2×5 grid → mapper detects percent-list, emits a 10-bar 3D chart. No human authoring step.

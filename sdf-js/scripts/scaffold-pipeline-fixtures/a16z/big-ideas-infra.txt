Big Ideas 2026: Infrastructure and security

Enterprises are drowning in unstructured multimodal data — PDFs, screenshots, videos — requiring continuous cleaning and structuring. Cybersecurity faces persistent hiring challenges: unfilled positions grew from under 1 million in 2013 to 3 million by 2021, which AI automation promises to resolve by eliminating repetitive log review. Agent-native infrastructure becomes essential as recursive, bursty workloads replace predictable human-speed traffic — a single agentic goal can trigger a recursive fan-out of 5,000 sub-tasks in milliseconds. Video generation shifts from passive consumption to interactive environments. Healthcare identifies healthy MAUs — preventatively-oriented consumers — as an underserved segment. The first AI-native university will adapt courses, advising, and assessment in real time.

Leaders, gainers and unexpected winners in the Enterprise AI arms race

78% of surveyed enterprise CIOs are using OpenAI models in production. Since May 2025, Anthropic posted the largest share increase of any frontier lab, growing 25% in enterprise penetration. 44% of enterprises now use Anthropic in production, rising to over 63% including testing. OpenAI commands ~56% wallet share. Over the last 2 years, average enterprise AI spend on LLMs rose from ~$4.5M to ~$7M, with enterprises expecting ~65% growth to ~$11.6M this year. 81% now use three or more model families, up from 68% less than a year ago. 75% of Anthropic customers had Sonnet 4.5 or Opus 4.5 in production, compared to 46% of OpenAI's customers on GPT 5.2 or 5.2 Pro. Microsoft 365 Copilot leads enterprise chat; GitHub Copilot remains the coding leader. 65% of enterprises preferred incumbent solutions, citing trust and integration.

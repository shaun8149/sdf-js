The AI Application Spending Report: Where Startup Dollars Really Go

Horizontal companies make up 60% of the list, compared to 40% for vertical companies. The analysis examined spending across Mercury's database of over 200,000 customers during June-August 2025. Creative tools represent the largest single category with ten companies on the list, led by Freepik at #4 and ElevenLabs at #5. Of the seventeen vertical application companies, twelve focus on supercharging humans while five aim to serve as AI employees. Nearly 70% of companies can be adopted by individuals, and 12 companies also appeared on the Consumer Top 100 list, with 11 having started as individual products.

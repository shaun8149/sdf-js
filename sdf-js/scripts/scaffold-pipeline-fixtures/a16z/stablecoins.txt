Big Ideas 2026: Stablecoins go mainstream

Stablecoins accounted for an estimated 46 trillion dollars in transaction volume last year, constantly hitting new all-time highs. That is more than 20x the volume of PayPal; close to 3x the volume of Visa, one of the largest payment networks in the world; and rapidly approaching the volume of ACH in the United States, the electronic network for financial transactions like direct deposits. Today, you can send a stablecoin in less than a second for less than a cent. In 2026, platforms will be built for wealth accumulation, not just wealth preservation, as fintechs like Revolut and Robinhood and exchanges like Coinbase leverage their tech-stack lead.

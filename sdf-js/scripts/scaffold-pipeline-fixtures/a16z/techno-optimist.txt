The Techno-Optimist Manifesto

Marc Andreessen argues technology and free markets drive human progress and material abundance. The techno-capital machine combines innovation with market forces, creating upward spirals of growth through falling prices and expanding purchasing power. Intelligence and energy are the cornerstone resources: AI as universal problem-solver, nuclear fission and fusion as abundant zero-emission energy. He envisions planetary population expanding to 50 billion people and settlement of other planets. Enemies include stagnation, central planning, bureaucracy, the Precautionary Principle, and deceleration. He advocates merit, ambition, bravery, and slouching toward Utopia through incremental improvement.

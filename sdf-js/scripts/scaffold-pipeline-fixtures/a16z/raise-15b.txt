Why Are We Here? Why Did We Raise $15B?

Andreessen Horowitz raised over $15B across multiple funds: American Dynamism ($1.176B), Apps ($1.7B), Bio + Health ($700M), Infrastructure ($1.7B), Growth ($6.75B), and other strategies ($3B) — representing over 18% of all U.S. venture capital allocated in 2025. Ben Horowitz frames the mission as providing opportunity regardless of background. America's system has produced unprecedented human flourishing over 250 years. The firm positions itself as responsible for American technological leadership in AI and crypto, applied to biology, health, defense, and education, warning that losing technological dominance risks economic, military, geopolitical, and cultural leadership as competition with China intensifies.

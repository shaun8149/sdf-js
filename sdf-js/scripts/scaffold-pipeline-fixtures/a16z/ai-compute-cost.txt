Navigating the High Cost of AI Compute

Generative AI faces compute demand exceeding supply by approximately 10 times. Access to compute at the lowest total cost has become a determining factor for AI companies, with many startups spending over 80% of capital on compute. GPT-3 has 175 billion parameters and costs between $500,000 and $4.6 million per training run on NVIDIA A100 GPUs, requiring over one terabyte of memory. A100 instance pricing ranges from roughly $0.25 to $1 per hour with nearly 4x differences among vendors. AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud charge premium rates while Coreweave and Lambda Labs offer lower costs. Startups should use cloud rather than build data centers unless infrastructure spending exceeds $50 million annually. Training datasets already use approximately 10% of all web pages via Common Crawl.

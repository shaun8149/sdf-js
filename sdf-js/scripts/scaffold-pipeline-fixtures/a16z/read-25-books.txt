You can just read 25 books

Andreessen Horowitz compiled book recommendations from 14 founders and partners, including leaders from Flock Safety, Applied Intuition, Decagon, Databricks, Kalshi, and Abridge. Featured titles span business strategy (Sam Walton: Made in America, Good to Great, Who), historical narratives (The Wright Brothers, Napoleon: A Life, Endurance), decision-making frameworks (Decisive, Monetizing Innovation), and philosophy (Man's Search for Meaning, In Praise of Shadows, The Magic Mountain). Ali Ghodsi recommended six books including The Effective Executive and Competing Against Luck. Reading approximately 25 books can help outsiders compete with established experts.
